How LGP Works?

Data Collection and Analytics

To train our algorithms for each client, we are finding a data set of the audience that makes conversions in this area and the behavior of these users on the network. The main task of the algorithm is to find similar patterns of audience in that segment
Building Machine Learning

Our machine learning models train from the user's response to communication across each ad channel. For each model, we consider thousands of parameters, such as psych types of users, weather conditions, news in the region
Spreading information to users

The process of buying ads is also fully automated. Places for displaying creatives are selected based on our forecasts of the influence of the channel on the user's reaction and the probability of conversion
